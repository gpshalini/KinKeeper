[DEMO SCRIPT - KINKEEPER DEMONSTRATION]
[Length: ~4.0 minutes]
[Perspective: First person, conversational, clear]

0:00 - 0:25 [Hook]
"Hi everyone! Have you ever missed a family birthday or anniversary because the reminder was buried in a busy chat group? We all have. But uploading our private family chats to public cloud databases just to extract calendar events is a massive privacy risk. That's why I built KinKeeper."

0:25 - 0:55 [What KinKeeper is]
"KinKeeper is a local-first, privacy-safe family memory concierge. It runs entirely on your local machine, allowing you to upload your chat exports and automatically extract upcoming birthdays, anniversaries, and remembrance days. It redacts phone numbers, cleans prompt injections, and designs beautiful reminder schedules without storing a single word of your raw chat messages."

0:55 - 1:15 [Show cover banner & diagrams]
"If we look at our new pastel project banner, we see our core promise: 'Never forget to remember the people you love.' Our system architecture maps the local UI to an ADK Orchestrator Workflow. The orchestrator routes data through a series of specialized LlmAgent sub-agents—an Event Extractor, an Identity Resolver to merge duplicate dates and nicknames, and a Creative Concierge. All filesystem reads and database saves are handled locally by our local Model Context Protocol server. No raw chat is ever stored, and no auto-sends or purchases can ever happen without your direct approval."

1:15 - 2:00 [Show live UI & paste chat]
"Let's jump into the live dashboard. I'll open my browser to localhost port 8000. Here is our dashboard! It features a beautiful, sage-green theme. Let's load the demo sample chat export file. I can do that by clicking 'Load Demo Sample'—which loads a synthetic WhatsApp-style log—and then clicking 'Analyze Memories'. Instantly, the system executes the multi-agent pipeline."

2:00 - 2:35 [Show weekly alerts & cards]
"Look at that! We have our upcoming memories neatly organized in rounded cards. Uncle Robert's birthday, Olivia's birthday, Lisa and David's anniversary, and Grandpa James's remembrance day. The system standardized 'Liv' to 'Olivia' and merged two duplicate birthday wishes into one single card! And look at the top: we have our new 'Happening this Week' alert panel, showing countdowns like 'Tomorrow!' and 'In 4 days' for the most imminent milestones."

2:35 - 3:10 [Show offline fallback]
"But what happens if the Gemini API rate limits are hit? If the LLM throws a 429 quota exception, KinKeeper doesn't crash. It immediately triggers our local regex-based offline fallback extractor. It extracts the events and updates the UI on-the-fly, ensuring that the app remains fully functional offline."

3:10 - 3:40 [Show privacy audit & tests]
"Now, let's scroll down to the Privacy Audit section. The system displays a live audit summary. We can see exactly how many messages were parsed, how many media attachments were skipped, how many phone numbers were redacted, and if any unsafe content was blocked. Behind the scenes, running 'make test' executes 30 unit and BDD tests in under 6 seconds, and running 'make eval' runs 10 evaluation test cases, proving that the system is 100% robust and secure."

3:40 - End [Close with impact]
"KinKeeper demonstrates how we can build powerful, multi-agent AI assistants that improve our daily lives while keeping our most personal communications completely safe, local, and private. Thank you!"
